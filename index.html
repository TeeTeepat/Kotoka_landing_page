Why Kotoka

The way we teach languages
is fundamentally broken

Language apps drill words in isolation — disconnected from the real world. Kotoka changes that.

The Problem
  • Words learned in apps are forgotten within days
  • No connection between vocabulary and real-life context
  • Repetitive drills kill motivation
  • Language stays in the app — never in your life
  • One-size-fits-all content ignores your actual environment

The Kotoka Solution
  • Snap & Learn — point your camera, get words from your real world
  • Memory Map — pin vocabulary to actual places you visit
  • Living Luggage — watch mastery grow like a travel collection
  • AI Chat Tutor — practice conversation with Koko, your AI guide
  • Spaced repetition — SM-2 algorithm ensures perfect review timing
